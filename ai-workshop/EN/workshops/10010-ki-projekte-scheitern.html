10010 · AI Readiness

Why 80% of AI projects fail before the first prompt.

Why will your AI initiative probably fail within six months? Because technology is rarely the first problem.

Reality Check

Core idea

Today's AI technologies will be replaced quickly. What matters is whether an organization can integrate AI into its work in a controlled, useful and sustainable way.

  • Understand AI readiness realistically
  • Structure data, knowledge and documentation
  • Introduce local and cloud AI with clear boundaries
  • Clarify governance, privacy and accountability early
  • Create practical value in everyday work

Typical risk

A pilot works, but the organization learns nothing from it.

Outcome

A clear view of prerequisites, blockers and meaningful next steps.

Description

This workshop is for organizations that want to introduce AI beyond experiments. It separates technology questions from organizational prerequisites such as knowledge quality, responsibility, data structure, privacy and operational value.
