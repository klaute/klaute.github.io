40040 · Local & Private AI

Using local AI systems securely and in line with privacy requirements.

From hardware and model choices to RAG and integration into engineering processes.

Private AI

Topics

  • Select suitable hardware and models
  • Evaluate local LLMs versus cloud services
  • Plan RAG systems on company knowledge
  • Protect privacy and confidentiality
  • Prepare integration into existing engineering workflows

Focus

Technical feasibility combined with security and privacy boundaries.

Outcome

A realistic target picture for local AI in your environment.

Description

This workshop clarifies when local models, private AI infrastructure and RAG systems are useful. Privacy, confidentiality, cost, operating models and integration effort are considered together.
