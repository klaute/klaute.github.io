50050 · AI Governance & Responsible AI

Using AI responsibly and in line with regulations.

Practical guardrails for privacy, information security, risk decisions and safe enterprise use.

Governance

Topics

  • Understand GDPR and the EU AI Act
  • Address privacy and information security
  • Understand risks and limits of generative AI
  • Decide when AI is useful and when it should not be used
  • Develop practical guidelines for safe enterprise adoption

Typical risk

Rules are created only after teams already use critical data in production workflows.

Outcome

Clear and practical guardrails for teams and decision makers.

Description

This workshop translates governance requirements into practical rules for technical organizations. It covers privacy, IP protection, information security, risk decisions and cases where AI should deliberately not be used.
