30030 · AI Readiness Assessment

Preparing organizations for meaningful AI adoption.

A pragmatic look at organization, data quality, documentation and real adoption blockers.

Assessment

Topics

  • Analyze the existing engineering and knowledge landscape
  • Assess data quality and documentation maturity
  • Identify technical and organizational blockers
  • Develop a pragmatic AI roadmap
  • Focus on sustainable adoption instead of short-lived pilots

Typical risk

AI is introduced before knowledge, data and responsibilities are ready.

Outcome

A reliable basis for decisions, investments and next steps.

Description

The assessment clarifies whether the organization has the prerequisites for useful AI adoption. It covers knowledge sources, documentation quality, processes, responsibilities and realistic use cases.
