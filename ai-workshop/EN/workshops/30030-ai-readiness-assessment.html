30030 · AI Strategy & Readiness

Preparing organizations for meaningful AI decisions.

A pragmatic look at models, cloud versus local deployment, privacy, infrastructure, use cases and real adoption blockers.

Strategy

Topics

  • Evaluate suitable models and operating models
  • Assess cloud versus local systems
  • Clarify privacy, confidentiality and infrastructure
  • Prioritize relevant use cases
  • Assess data quality and documentation maturity
  • Identify technical and organizational blockers
  • Develop a pragmatic AI roadmap

Typical risk

AI is introduced before knowledge, data and responsibilities are ready.

Outcome

A reliable basis for decisions, investments and next steps.

Description

This workshop clarifies which AI strategy fits the organization. It covers models, operating models, privacy, infrastructure, knowledge sources, processes, responsibilities and realistic use cases.
