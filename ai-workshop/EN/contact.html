What the first contact is about

An initial conversation is non-binding. The first step is to understand your technical situation: the existing system or platform architecture, open architecture decisions, integration problems, security boundaries or questions around AI-assisted engineering.

I do not begin a collaboration with a predefined format. Once the problem, context and expected outcome are clear, we can determine whether a workshop, architecture review, assessment or another form of technical support is appropriate.

Contact Options

Typical Next Step

After your message, we clarify the goal, context and open technical questions. We can then determine whether a workshop, architecture review, assessment or focused architecture or security engagement is the appropriate next step.
