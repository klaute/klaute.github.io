What the first contact is about

An initial conversation is non-binding. The first step is to understand your technical situation: where AI is already being used, which questions are open and whether a workshop or another format makes sense.

I do not start a collaboration with a workshop. I start by understanding your technical situation. Once it is clear where AI can create real value, we can decide together which format fits.

Contact Options

The easiest way to reach me is by email or LinkedIn. This website does not use a contact form.

Typical Next Step

After your message, we usually clarify goals, context and open questions. If a workshop is useful, we select the fitting format together. If another type of support is more appropriate, that can be discussed as well.
