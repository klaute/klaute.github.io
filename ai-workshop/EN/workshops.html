AI Workshop Portfolio 07/2026

Integrating AI into engineering organizations safely, usefully and sustainably.

I help engineering organizations introduce AI as a structured way of working: with clear understanding, reliable prerequisites, explicit responsibility and practical value.

Why these workshops?

Companies often believe they need AI. In reality, they first need to learn how to make their organization AI-ready. Today's technologies will change; sustainable adoption depends on knowledge, data, responsibility and useful workflows.

Working Logic

  1. Understand
  2. Create structure
  3. Build prerequisites
  4. Use technology deliberately
