AI Workshop Portfolio 07/2026

A workshop portfolio for AI in engineering.

The workshops address different maturity levels: from the first reality check through strategy, secure AI infrastructure and governance to embedding AI-assisted engineering in teams and processes.

What this is about

Many companies start with tools. AI becomes sustainable in engineering when use cases, data, security, architecture, reviews and responsibility are considered together.

Orientation

  1. Where are we now?
  2. What is technically and organizationally viable?
  3. Which risks need boundaries?
  4. How does AI become part of daily engineering work?

Which Entry Point Fits?

If you are not yet sure where AI is useful, start with readiness and strategy. If architecture, privacy or local models are central, Secure AI Engineering is the right entry point. If developers already use AI or are expected to use it, AI-assisted Engineering should come first.
