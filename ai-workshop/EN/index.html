Why workshops?

Many AI initiatives do not fail because of the model. They fail because use cases are unclear, knowledge is unstructured, review is missing and responsibility is undefined.

Systems Architecture

AI becomes useful when tasks, architecture context, quality boundaries and technical decisions are deliberately guided.

Security & Privacy

Source code, internal knowledge and customer data need clear rules: cloud or local, RAG, access, review and secure integration.

Engineering Enablement

Teams do not need a prompt collection. They need reliable working practices: planning, coding rules, reviews and traceable decisions.

Do not introduce ChatGPT. Improve engineering work.

The value does not come from another tool. It comes from structure: suitable use cases, prepared data, clear architecture, security boundaries and people who can review the result. This is where my work connects systems architecture, security, AI engineering and engineering enablement.
