Approach

AI as a Structured Engineering Tool

AI should not be treated as magic.

Productive use of AI requires:

  • Problem decomposition
  • Context definition
  • Structured workflows
  • Verification and review
  • Understanding of limitations

The focus is not on replacing engineers, but on augmenting structured technical thinking and analysis.
